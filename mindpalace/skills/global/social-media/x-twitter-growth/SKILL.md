# X / Twitter Growth Engine

X-specific depth: algorithm mechanics, thread engineering, reply strategy, profile optimization, competitive intel.

## Step 1 — Profile audit
**Bio**: clear value prop in line 1 (who you help + how), specific niche (not "entrepreneur | thinker | builder"), social proof, CTA/link, no hashtags. **Pinned**: exists, <30 days old, best work, clear CTA. **Recent activity (last 30 posts)**: ≥1x/day (ideal 3-5x), format mix, reply ratio >30%, engagement trend.

## Step 2 — Competitive intel
Search `site:x.com "topic" min_faves:100` to find high performers. Pick 5-10 niche accounts; extract hook patterns, top 3-5 themes, format mix (tweet/thread/reply/quote), posting times, engagement triggers (reply vs like vs RT).

## Step 3 — Content creation (by growth impact)
1. **Threads** (highest reach + follow conversion): Tweet 1 = hook (stop scroll <7 words); Tweet 2 = context/promise; Tweets 3-N = one idea each, standalone-worthy; final = summary + explicit CTA; reply to T1 restates hook + "Follow for more." Rules: 5-12 tweets, each readable alone, line breaks, 3-4 lines max, number them or use "↓".
2. **Atomic tweets** (breadth): observation / listicle / contrarian / lesson / framework. <200 chars, one idea, NO links in body (link in reply), questions drive replies.
3. **Quote tweets** (authority): original + your unique take (add data, counterpoint, experience). Never just "This."
4. **Replies** (fastest visibility): reply to accounts 2-10x your size, add real value, be early, make the reply tweet-worthy.

## Step 4 — Algorithm mechanics (2025-2026)
Rewards (high→low): replies received (very high) > read time > profile visits from tweet > bookmarks > retweets/quotes > likes > link clicks (penalized). **Kills reach**: links in body (use first reply), editing within 30m, posting then going offline, >2 hashtags, tagging non-engagers, inconsistent thread quality.

Cadence by size: <1K → 2-3 tweets/day, 1-2 threads/wk, 10-20 replies/day. 1K-10K → 3-5, 2-3, 5-15. 10K-50K → 3-7, 2-4, 5-10. 50K+ → 2-5, 1-3, 5-10.

## Step 5 — Growth playbook
- **Week 1-2 (foundation)**: optimize bio + pinned; pick 20 niche accounts; reply 10-20x/day (value only); 2-3 atomic tweets/day testing formats; 1 thread.
- **Week 3-4 (pattern recognition)**: double down on top 2 formats; 3-5 posts/day; 2-3 threads/wk; daily quote-tweets.
- **Month 2+ (scale)**: 3-5 recurring series (e.g. "Friday Framework"); repurpose threads → LinkedIn/newsletter; build mutual-engagement relationships with 5-10 peers; experiment with Spaces.

## Common pitfalls
Links in body · weak thread tweet 1 · inconsistent posting · only broadcasting (replies = 50%+ of growth) · generic bio · copying formats without adapting to your niche.
