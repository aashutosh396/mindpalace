# Social Content Creation

Create engaging, platform-native content. Always write the hook first — everything else is worthless if the first line doesn't land.

## Gather context
Goals (awareness/leads/traffic/community + desired action + personal vs company brand) · audience (who, which platforms, what they engage with) · brand voice (tone, topics to avoid, terminology) · resources (time, repurposable content, video capacity).

## Platform quick reference
| Platform | Best for | Frequency | Key format |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | B2B, thought leadership | 3-5x/wk | carousels, stories |
| Twitter/X | tech, real-time | 3-10x/day | threads, hot takes |
| Instagram | visual brands | 1-2 posts + stories daily | reels, carousels |
| TikTok | awareness, young | 1-4x/day | short video |
| Facebook | communities, local | 1-2x/day | groups, native video |

## Content pillars (3-5)
Example for a SaaS founder: industry insights 30% · behind-the-scenes 25% · educational 25% · personal/hot takes 15% · promotional 5%. For each pillar ask: unique perspective? audience questions? past winners? sustainable? business-aligned?

## Hook formulas
- **Curiosity**: "I was wrong about [belief]." / "The real reason [outcome] isn't what you think."
- **Story**: "Last week, [unexpected thing] happened." / "3 years ago I [past]. Today, [present]."
- **Value**: "How to [outcome] (without [pain]):" / "[N] [things] that [outcome]:" / "Stop [mistake]. Do this instead:"
- **Contrarian**: "Unpopular opinion: [bold statement]" / "[Common advice] is wrong. Here's why:"

## Repurposing system
Blog → LinkedIn insight (link in comments), carousel, Twitter thread, IG carousel, IG reel. Workflow: create pillar content → extract 3-5 insights → adapt per platform → schedule across week → update/reshare evergreen.

## Batching (2-3 hrs weekly)
Review pillar topics → write 5 LinkedIn posts → 3 threads + daily tweets → IG carousel + reel ideas → schedule → leave room for real-time engagement.

## Engagement (daily 30 min)
Respond to all comments (5m) · comment on 5-10 target posts (15m) · share with added insight (5m) · 2-3 DMs (5m). Quality comments add insight/experience/follow-up — never "Great post!" Build 20-50 target relationships over time → collaborate.

## Analytics & optimization
Awareness: impressions, reach, follower growth. Engagement: rate, comments (> likes), shares, saves. Conversion: link clicks, profile visits, DMs, attributed leads. Weekly: top 3 and bottom 3 posts (why), trends, best times.
- Low engagement → test hooks, times, formats; engage more.
- Declining reach → avoid external links in body, post more, engage in comments, try video.

## Scheduling
Schedule core/evergreen content; post live for real-time commentary and responses. Keep 1-2 weeks queued, review weekly, leave gaps.

## Proactive flags
Same content across platforms → adapt per platform. No hook → write it first. Unsustainable frequency → focus 1-2 platforms + batch. Promo >20% → rebalance to value/story. No engagement strategy → offer the daily routine.

## Output
Platform-native post (hook + body + CTA + hashtag recs) · content calendar (topic/platform/format/pillar/day) · repurposing plan (1 source → 5-8 derivatives) · 5 hook variants · full thread structure. Always lead with a hook; flag evergreen vs timely.
