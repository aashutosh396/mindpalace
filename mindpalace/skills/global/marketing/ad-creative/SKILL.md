# Ad Creative Production

Pure creative production (distinct from campaign strategy). Produce copy that passes review, stops the scroll, and drives action — at scale.

## Gather context
Product & offer (specific: product/feature/trial/lead magnet, value prop in one sentence) · audience (job title, pain, objections, beliefs) · platform & stage (which platform, funnel stage, iterate vs fresh) · performance data if iterating (current copy, winners, CTR/CVR/CPA, what's tested).

## Modes
1. **Generate from scratch** — extract core message → map to funnel stage → select framework → 5-10 headlines per formula → body per platform → quality checks.
2. **Iterate from data** — audit current angles → identify winning pattern → 3-5 on-theme variations → 2-3 new-angle tests → validate.
3. **Scale variations** — lock core message → vary one element at a time → adapt across platforms → creative matrix (angles × platforms).

## Platform specs (key limits)
Google RSA: 30-char headlines ×15, 90-char descriptions ×4, max 3 pinned. Google Display: 30×5 headlines, 90×5 descriptions + 5 images. Meta: 40-char headline, 125-char primary text, minimal image text. LinkedIn: 70-char headline, 150-char intro, no clickbait. X: 70-char, 280 total. TikTok: 80-100 char caption, hook in first 3s.

## Framework by funnel stage
- **Awareness — lead with the problem**: Problem → Amplify → Hint at Solution. Lead with pain, their language, don't pitch. (Curiosity, stat, "you know that feeling" hooks.)
- **Consideration — lead with the solution**: Solution → Mechanism → Proof. Explain through the outcome lens; the mechanism matters; social proof starts mattering. (Benefit-first, comparison, how-it-works.)
- **Decision — lead with proof**: Proof → Risk Removal → Urgency. Testimonials/results with numbers, free trial/money-back/no credit card, only real urgency. (Social proof, guarantee-first, before/after.)

## Headline formulas
- **Benefit-first**: `[Verb] [specific outcome] [timeframe]` — "Cut churn 30% without chasing customers."
- **Curiosity**: surprising/counterintuitive — "Why your best customers leave at 90 days."
- **Social proof**: `[Number] [companies] [outcome]` — "1,200 SaaS teams use this to cut support tickets."
- **Urgency (done right)**: real scarcity — "Q1 pricing ends March 31." (No "🔥 LIMITED TIME!!!" — rejected + desperate.)
- **Problem agitation**: vivid pain — "Still losing 40% of signups before they see value?"

## Iteration methodology
Diagnose the winner (hook type, funnel stage, emotional driver, CTA ask) → extract the pattern (specific numbers? customer voice? direct benefit?) → generate 3-5 on-theme variations → test one new angle (2-3 ads) → validate and submit.

## Quality checklist
**Compliance**: char counts within limits · no ALL CAPS (except acronyms) · no excessive punctuation · no "click here"/"buy now"/platform trademarks · no first-person platform references. **Quality**: headline stands alone · specific over vague · CTA matches landing page · no unbackable claims (#1, best-in-class). **Audience**: would the ICP stop scrolling? matches their language? right funnel stage?

## Proactive flags
Generic headlines ("Grow your business") → replace with measurable. Char violations → flag clearly. Stage-message mismatch → adjust. Fake urgency → flag trust + rejection risk. No hook variety → flag testing gap. Copy lifted verbatim from landing page → flag (connected, not identical).

Tag each line 🟢 tested formula / 🟡 new angle / 🔴 high-risk claim, with char counts visible.
