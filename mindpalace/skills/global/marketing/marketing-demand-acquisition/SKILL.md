Acquisition playbook calibrated for Series A+ B2B SaaS scaling internationally (EU/US/Canada, hybrid PLG/Sales-Led). Adapt benchmarks for other stages/motions.

## Core KPIs
Demand gen: MQL/SQL volume, cost per opportunity, marketing-sourced pipeline $, MQL→SQL rate. Paid: CAC, ROAS, CPL, CPA, channel efficiency. SEO: organic sessions, non-brand %, rankings, technical health. Partnerships: partner-sourced pipeline, partner CAC, co-marketing ROI.

## Demand gen funnel
TOFU (paid social, display, content syndication, SEO → awareness) → MOFU (paid search, retargeting, gated content, nurture → MQLs/demos) → BOFU (brand search, outreach, case studies, trials → SQLs/pipeline).
Campaign workflow: objective+budget+audience → channels by stage → build in CRM with UTMs → lead scoring + assignment → launch with test budget + validate tracking.
**UTM:** `utm_source` (linkedin/google/meta) · `utm_medium` (cpc/display/email) · `utm_campaign` (qN-yyyy-channel-segment) · `utm_content` (variant) · `utm_term` (paid search only).

## Paid channels
| Channel | Best for | CAC | Priority |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | B2B/Enterprise/ABM | $150-400 | High |
| Google Search | High-intent BOFU | $80-250 | High |
| Google Display | Retargeting | $50-150 | Medium |
| Meta | SMB/visual | $60-200 | Medium |

LinkedIn: Awareness→Consideration→Conversion campaigns, target Director+/50-5000 emp, start $50/day, scale 20%/wk if CAC<target. Google: Brand→Competitor→Solution→Category keyword priority, 5-10 tightly-themed kws/ad group, 3 RSAs each, 100+ negatives, Manual CPC → Target CPA after 50+ conversions.
Sample $40k/mo split: LinkedIn $15k (10 SQL) · Google Search $12k (20) · Display $5k (5) · Meta $5k (8) · Partnerships $3k (5).

## SEO
Technical foundation: sitemap submitted, robots.txt, HTTPS, mobile speed >90, Core Web Vitals passing, structured data, canonicals, hreflang. Keyword tiers: BOFU high-intent (first) → MOFU solution-aware → TOFU problem-aware. On-page: kw in URL/title(60ch)/meta(155ch)/H1, 2000-3000 words for comprehensive topics, 3-5 internal links. Links: digital PR, guest posts (DA 40+), partner co-marketing, community.

## Partnerships
Tiers: strategic integrations (high ROI) → affiliate → customer referrals → marketplace listings. Workflow: find overlapping-ICP non-competing partners → outreach with specific proposal → define metrics/revenue/term → co-branded assets + tracking → enable partner sales. Affiliate: platform (PartnerStack/Impact/Rewardful), 20-30% recurring commission, enablement kit, recruit.

## Attribution
First-Touch (awareness) / Last-Touch (direct response) / W-Shaped 40-20-40 (hybrid PLG/Sales — recommended). 90-day lookback. Weekly dashboard targets: MQL→SQL >15%, blended CAC <$300, pipeline velocity <60 days.

## MQL→SQL handoff
SQL criteria: Director+/budget authority, 50-5000 emp, $10k+ annual, buying within 90 days, demo requested. SLA: SDR responds to MQL in 4h, AE books demo with SQL in 24h, first demo in 3 business days.

## Channel benchmarks (B2B SaaS Series A)
CTR — LinkedIn 0.4-0.9% · Google 2-5% · SEO 1-3% · Email 15-25%. CVR — 1-3% / 3-7% / 2-5% / 2-5%.

## Proactive flags
Over-relying on one channel · no lead scoring · CAC exceeding LTV · no nurture for non-ready leads (80% aren't ready to buy).
