Build a referral system that actually runs — right mechanics, triggers, incentives, measurement. Distinguish customer referral from affiliate.

## Modes
Design new program · optimize existing (diagnose the broken loop stage, fix one lever, measure, repeat) · launch affiliate program.

## Referral vs affiliate
| | Customer referral | Affiliate |
|---|---|---|
| Who promotes | Existing customers | External partners/publishers/influencers |
| Motivation | Loyalty, reward, social currency | Commission, audience alignment |
| Best for | B2C, prosumer, SMB SaaS | B2B SaaS, high LTV, content niches |
| Activation | Triggered by aha/milestone | Recruited + onboarded |
| Payout | Credit/discount/cash | Revenue share or flat fee |
Rule: enthusiastic social customers → start with referrals; team-buying businesses → start with affiliates.

## The referral loop (4 stages — any weak stage breaks it)
`[Trigger Moment] → [Share Action] → [Referred User Converts] → [Reward Delivered] → loop`
1. **Trigger** — ask after the aha moment / milestone / great support / renewal / public win. NOT day 1, NOT onboarding emails, NOT email footers.
2. **Share** — pre-filled editable message, personal link (not generic code), email/link/social/Slack options, mobile-optimized, one-click.
3. **Converts** — personalized landing ("Your friend Alex invited you"), incentive visible, attribution tracked landing→conversion, clear CTA.
4. **Reward** — confirm eligibility at signup (not at payment), notify referrer immediately, show status in dashboard.

## Incentive design
Single-sided (referrer only; use when viral hooks strong / referral rate >5%) vs double-sided (both; overcomes inertia; use when referral rate <1%). Reward types: account credit (SaaS), discount (ecom), cash (high-LTV B2C, cap 30% of first payment), feature unlock (freemium), status (community), charity (mission-driven). Sizing: account credit ≥10% of first month's value. Tiered (max 3 levels, each meaningfully better): 1→$20, 3→$75+bonus, 10→$300+ambassador status.

## Optimization levers (diagnose first)
| Metric | Benchmark | If below |
|---|---|---|
| Program awareness | >40% of active users | Promote in-app + post-activation emails |
| Active referrers | 5-15% | Improve triggers + visibility |
| Share rate | 20-40% of those who see it | Simplify flow, improve messaging |
| Referred conversion | 15-25% (vs 5-10% organic) | Improve landing page + incentive |
| Reward redemption | >70% in 30 days | Reduce friction, send reminders |

## Key metrics
Referral rate (sent/active) · active referrers % · referral conversion rate · CAC via referral (reward cost/new customers) · referral revenue contribution · **virality coefficient K = referrals/user × conversion rate** (K>1 = viral growth).

## Affiliate launch checklist
Commission (% revenue or flat), cookie window (30 days min, 90 for B2B), tracking platform (Impact/ShareASale/Rewardful/PartnerStack), agreement (legal review), payment terms · partner kit (unique links, copy/swipes, banners, product sheet, optimized landing) · recruit (50 targets, personalized outreach, 10-affiliate pilot before scaling).

## Proactive flags
Asking at signup · reward <5% of LTV with low rate · no instant reward notification · generic share message · attribution stops at landing page · affiliate program with no partner kit.
