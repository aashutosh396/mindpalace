# Content Strategy

Plan content that drives traffic, builds authority, and generates leads by being searchable, shareable, or both.

## Gather context
- **Business**: what the company does, ICP, primary content goal (traffic/leads/awareness/thought leadership), problems the product solves.
- **Customer research**: pre-buy questions, sales-call objections, recurring support topics, customer language.
- **Current state**: existing content + what works, resources (writers/budget/time), producible formats.
- **Competitive**: main competitors, content gaps.

## Searchable vs Shareable (core classification per topic)
- **Searchable** — people already query it (keyword volume). Goal: rank + convert. Formats: use-case pages, comparisons, how-tos, hub/spoke clusters. Judged by rankings + organic conversions over 6-12 months.
- **Shareable** — nobody searches it yet but it spreads (original data, contrarian POV, narrative). Goal: reach + links + brand. Judged by distribution (shares, referral, backlinks) in the first weeks.

**Decision rule**: meaningful volume + buyer intent → searchable (build into a cluster). No volume but a distribution hook → shareable (plan the launch channel before writing). Both → searchable structure with a shareable angle (best ROI). Neither → don't write it.

## Topic cluster mapping
Once priority topics exist, group them — but verify cluster boundaries by buyer-stage/intent coherence, not just lexical similarity.

## Output format
1. **Content pillars** — 3-5 with rationale, subtopic clusters each, product connection.
2. **Priority topics** — per piece: topic/title, searchable/shareable/both, content type, target keyword + buyer stage, customer-research backing.
3. **Topic cluster map** — structured view of how content interconnects.

## Diagnostic questions
Patterns from last 10 customer conversations? Recurring sales-call questions? Where competitors' content falls short? Unique research insights not shared elsewhere? Which existing content converts best, and why?

## Proactive flags
No plan → propose a 3-pillar starter with 10 seed topics. Content but low traffic → flag searchable/shareable imbalance. Writing without keyword target → warn effort may be wasted. Too many audiences → flag ICP dilution; split by persona/use-case. Competitor outranks on core topics → gap analysis + quick wins.
